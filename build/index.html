Wireless energy transmission has a wide range of applications in consumer electronics, automotive, medical and many other industries. In addition, in the consumer sector, wireless energy transmission is actively used to charge smartphones, tablets, wearable devices and other electronics.
Today, Node company offers the market a number of technical solutions in the field of wireless energy transmission - from consumer electronics to industrial products.

OUR DEVICES ARE:

  • Convenient location choice
    Less wires and more space saving.
  • Ergonomics
    It is possible to transfer energy to many devices with any power simultaneously.
  • Economical
    Low to medium cost, in comparison with other solutions, energy conversion efficiency (90%).
  • Versatility
    Technology can be applied to any electronics.
  • Design
    Stylish, ergonomic and modern design. Convenient use and storage.
The process of creating products using technologies from Node passed the stage from experimental studies to the creation and successful final testing of prototype devices. Our investors get an opportunity not only to increase their capital, but also to become the first owners of such devices:
  • Alpha

    Flexible and stylish pad for wireless charging of electronic devices.

    A large area of the charging surface allows you to charge several devices, placing them side by side or even on each other.

    Specifications:

    • - Surface area 300x200mm
    • - Transmitted power up to 10W
    • - USB power supply 5V
  • Eon

    Wireless charging station for desktop placement.

    It has small dimensions and is capable of transmitting energy within a radius of up to 1 meter.

    The device provides charging of several devices simultaneously and is equipped with automatic power control.

    Specifications:

    • - Power supply- 220V
    • - Dimensions - 100x70mm

The NODE tokens that a company will acquire as a result of a buyback will be distributed among existing investors in proportion to the current number of tokens on the balance at the time of such distribution.

Annual buyback of tokens will be calculated using the following formula: "Volume of undistributed profit*3*Volume of the NODE tokens sold/100%"

That is, if the sales level of 10 million NODE tokens is reached, 30% of the undistributed profit will be redeemed, followed by a proportional distribution of tokens between the remaining investors.

Therefore, an investor can already calculate the guaranteed interest income that he will receive annually from the buyout of tokens, regardless of the demand for the NODE tokens on the market. Accordingly, the longer an investor holds the tokens, the more income he can get.

At any given time, the buyer will have the opportunity to purchase NODE products with a 25% discount in relation to the value in a fiat currency (USD) with a condition of payment with the company's tokens. For example, with the actual cost of the Alpha at 99.0USD, the purchase price when paying with NODE tokens will be 74.25 USD. The tokens that the company will acquire as a result of the sale of its products will be burned, thus reducing their total quantity in a turnover.
